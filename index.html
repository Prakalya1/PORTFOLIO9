Recent Projects

SELF IDENTIFYING MENTAL HEALTH AND GET GUIDENCE FOR SUPPORT

The "Self-Identifying Mental Health" web application is designed to empower users in understanding their mental health status through interactive assessments and personalized feedback. By utilizing evidence-based questionnaires, the app helps individuals identify potential mental health concerns and offers tailored resources for support and guidance. Users can track their mental well-being over time, fostering awareness and encouraging proactive steps toward self-care.

POLAR CODED OFDM FOR UNDER WATER COMMUNICATION

The "Polar Coded OFDM for Underwater Communication" project aims to enhance the reliability and efficiency of data transmission in underwater environments, which are often characterized by high attenuation and multipath fading. By integrating polar coding with Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiplexing (OFDM), the system leverages the strengths of both technologies to improve error correction capabilities and optimize bandwidth utilization. The project focuses on developing a robust communication framework that can adapt to varying underwater conditions

Smart Traffic Management System

The "Smart Traffic Management System" project aims to optimize traffic flow and reduce congestion in urban areas through the use of advanced sensors, data analytics, and real-time monitoring. By integrating vehicle detection systems and traffic signal controls, the project enhances the efficiency of traffic lights based on real-time vehicle counts and traffic patterns. The system incorporates machine learning algorithms to predict traffic conditions and adjust signal timings accordingly, improving overall traffic flow and reducing waiting times.
